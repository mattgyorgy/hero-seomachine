## Current Pricing: Founder Pricing

### Plan: $79/month

| Inclusion | Limit |
|-----------|-------|
| Recording hours | 10 hours/month |
| Storage | 250 GB |
| Projects | Unlimited |
| Clients | Unlimited |
| Editing | Not included — $99 per project (see below) |

### Editing: $99 Per Project

- Editing is an add-on, charged per project at $99
- **All deliverables are included** in the $99 project editing fee — full episode, social clips, vertical shorts, whatever the Host requests
- Rush turnaround is available (pricing may vary)
- Standard turnaround: 3–5 business days

### Free Trial

- **14-day free trial** — full platform access, no credit card commitment
- **$99 video editing credit** included at no cost after completing the onboarding wizard
- This means new Hosts can try the complete workflow — recording, Smart Library, and one professionally edited project — before paying anything

### Founder Pricing Guarantee

- $79/month is **locked in forever** for early adopters
- This price will not increase for Hosts who sign up during the Founder Pricing period
- Future pricing for new sign-ups may be higher

### Plan Limits

- When a Host hits the 10-hour recording cap or 250GB storage limit, they need to upgrade
- Higher-tier plans with increased limits are coming — do not reference specific upgrade pricing or tiers until they're announced

- Per-project freelance editor: $300–500 per video

## Pricing Messaging Rules

### DO:
- Reference $79/mo and $99/project as exact numbers — they're public and accurate
- Use "Founder Pricing" to create urgency (implies price will increase for future sign-ups)
- Frame editing as a per-project add-on, not a hidden cost — transparency builds trust
- Emphasize "all deliverables included" in the $99 — this is a major value differentiator
- Use both ROI comparisons (freelance and production company) depending on audience
- Mention "unlimited projects and clients" — this is a strong signal for agencies

### DON'T:
- Say "unlimited editing" — editing is $99/project, not included in the base plan
- Reference upgrade pricing or tier details — these haven't been announced yet
- Imply unlimited recording or storage — there are 10hr/mo and 250GB caps
- Use "starting at" language — there's one plan, one price
- Fabricate competitor pricing — use the ranges provided ($300-500 freelance, $1K-5K production)
- Reference overage pricing — there are no overages, just a hard cap requiring upgrade

## Content Guidelines

- **Lead with the full picture:** "$79/mo for the platform + $99 per project for professional editing" — never bury the editing cost
- **Anchor against alternatives:** Always pair Hero Network pricing with the cost of the alternative (freelancers or production companies)
- **Highlight what's truly unlimited:** Projects and clients have no cap — this matters for agencies managing multiple accounts
- **Create urgency with Founder Pricing:** "Lock in $79/mo forever" is a legitimate, accurate urgency driver
- **Be honest about limits:** 10 hours of recording and 250GB storage are real constraints — frame them as generous for the price point, not unlimited
