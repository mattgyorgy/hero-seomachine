## Linking Guidelines

### When to Link Internally
- First natural mention of a topic covered by another article
- When referencing a feature or use case with a dedicated page
- When suggesting a next step that has a resource page
- CTAs at the end of articles — link to pricing or relevant related content

### When NOT to Over-Link
- Don't link to the same page more than 2x per article
- Don't force links — only link when it adds value for the reader
- Don't link from every keyword — pick the most meaningful placement

### Link Priority Order (highest to lowest)
1. Product/pricing pages — highest conversion value
2. Pillar content — SEO cluster authority
3. Cluster articles — topical authority
4. Related resources — reader journey
